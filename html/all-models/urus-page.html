lamborghini urus

crossover

The first crossover (SSUV - Super Sport Utility Vehicle) from the Italian car manufacturer Lamborghini. The car was unveiled on December 4, 2017 at the Lamborghini plant in Sant'Agata Bolognese. Unlike Lamborghini's usual naturally aspirated V10 and V12 engines, the Urus has a 4.0-liter twin-turbo V8 from Audi, and is also used in some of the Audi S and RS series and Audi A8, Bentley Continental V8 and Flying Spur V8. The Urus is much lighter than the same-platform Audi Q7, Bentley Bentayga, Porsche Cayenne and Volkswagen Touareg due to the use of carbon fiber in the construction.

  • Maximum power 650 horsepower
  • Drive unit permanent full
  • Maximum speed 305km / h
  • Acceleration 0 - 100 km / h in 3.6 s
  • The cost of the car is about $220,000
