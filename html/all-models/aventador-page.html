lamborghini aventador

sportcar

Sports car produced by Lamborghini since 2011. The Aventador replaces the Lamborghini Murciélago. As with the Murciélago, parent company Audi exploited the bullfighting theme. Lamborghini advertisements claimed that the Aventador was the famous bull raised by the sons of Don Celestino Cuadri Vides, who rose to fame after one of the bloodiest battles in Zaragoza, for which he received the prestigious Trofeo de la Peña La Madroñera award for outstanding bravery in the arena. The prefix LP 700-4 at the end of the name means the technical equipment of the car, where 700 reflects the power in hp, and 4 - all-wheel drive.

  • Equipped with a 6.5-liter 12-cylinder engine with a capacity of 700 horsepower
  • Designed by Felippo Perini
  • Develops a top speed of 350 km / h (218 mph)
  • Acceleration from 0 to 100 km / h takes 2.9 sec
  • The cost of the car is about $500,000
