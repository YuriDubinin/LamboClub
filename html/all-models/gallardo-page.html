lamborghini gallardo

sportcar

A sports car manufactured by Lamborghini from 2003 to 2013. Smaller and less powerful model of the company compared to the Lamborghini Murciélago. The presentation of the car took place at the Geneva Motor Show in March 2003. To date, this is the most massive model from Lamborghini - 14022 cars were built in 10 years of production. On November 25, 2013, the last instance was built - the Gallardo roadster in the LP570-4 Spyder Performante modification. The successor to the Gallardo is the Lamborghini Huracán unveiled to the public at the Geneva Motor Show in March 2014.

  • The name of the car comes from the Spanish breed of fighting bulls, which was bred by the cattle breeder Francisco Gaillardo in the 18th century
  • Installed a five-liter V10 engine with a capacity of 500 horsepower
  • Acceleration from 0 to 100 km / h took 4.2 seconds
  • Top speed was 309 km / h (192 mph)
  • The cost of the car is about $240,000
