lamborghini huracan

sportcar

Sports car produced by the Italian company Lamborghini, which replaced the Lamborghini Gallardo. It debuted at the Geneva Motor Show in March 2014. Huracán was the name of the ancient Maya god of wind, the same name was given to the fighting bull, which performed in bullfights at the end of the 19th century. As with the Pagani Huayra (Huayra is another windy deity from South America), Huracán is pronounced with the first letter skipped (i.e. Huracán). The full official name is Lamborghini Huracan LP 610-4. The last four digits traditionally indicate horsepower and the number of driven wheels.

  • Equipped with a 5.2-liter 10-cylinder naturally aspirated petrol engine with 610 horsepower
  • The place of the former Graziano gearbox is now replaced by a seven-speed LDF double-clutch transmission
  • Acceleration to 100 km / h occurs in 3.2 seconds
  • The maximum speed is 325 km / h
  • The cost of the car is about $330,000
