club

The creations of the Automobili Lamborghini brand are the passion of thousands of collectors and sports supercar owners around the world. Over the years, many of these enthusiastic owners have formed prestigious clubs to share their enthusiasm and proud sense of belonging to Automobili Lamborghini. The sense of belonging and enthusiasm of the members of these clubs helped to enhance the value of the Lamborghini brand, which decided to give the official clubs of each country a more active role by facilitating the organization of special events and exclusive events for the owners of cars produced by the automobile house in Sant'Agata Bolognese.
