Automobili

Lamborghini S.p.A., italian company, manufacturer of expensive sports cars under the Lamborghini brand. Located in the municipality of Sant'Agata Bolognese, near Bologna.

origins

There are several versions about the reasons why Lamborghini founded his own company. All these versions boil down to the conflict between Enzo Ferrari (the owner of the Ferrari company) and Ferruccio Lamborghini.

world

Official Lamborghini dealers are provided in Austria, Germany, Greece, Spain, Italy, Monaco, France, Switzerland, Romania, Turkey, Canada and the USA.
